# Content Pillars
## AI_SNIPP Research & Intelligence System

*The four pillars are derived from the brand's own end card: "AI Tools • AI Certifications • AI Updates • AI Cheat Codes". Every piece of content maps to exactly one pillar. If a topic doesn't fit any pillar, it doesn't belong on this account.*

---

## THE FOUR PILLARS

### Pillar 1 — AI TOOLS
*"The tool you don't know yet"*

**Definition:** Discovery, demonstration, and comparison of AI tools. The creator's role here is the scout — finding tools before the Indian AI audience does and running real demos so viewers don't waste time testing bad tools themselves.

**What belongs here:**
- New AI tools under 6 months of Indian market awareness
- Underrated tools that solve specific professional problems
- Free alternatives to expensive paid tools
- Hidden features within well-known tools (when the feature itself is tool-level depth)
- Indian-specific tools (vernacular AI, GST tools, tools with INR pricing)
- Side-by-side tool comparisons (same task, different tools)

**What does NOT belong here:**
- Generic "top 10 AI tools" roundups with no personal testing
- Tools the creator hasn't personally used
- Tools with no Indian relevance or accessibility

**Primary Formulas:** F04 (Hidden Tool), F03 (Hollywood VFX), F01 (AI Cheat Code when tool-specific)
**Backup Formulas:** F06 (AI vs Human — tool comparison variant)
**Primary CTA:** DM trigger ("TOOLS", "LIST")
**Primary Metric:** DM volume, Save rate

---

### Pillar 2 — AI CHEAT CODES
*"The shortcut most people don't know"*

**Definition:** Non-obvious shortcuts, workflows, prompt techniques, system configurations, and process improvements using AI. The creator's role here is the optimizer — showing viewers how to get dramatically more from AI tools they already have.

**What belongs here:**
- Prompt engineering tricks and system prompt configurations
- Workflow automations (3-step systems that save real time)
- Before/after transformations showing quality gap
- AI vs manual comparisons with real time numbers
- Hidden settings, lesser-known API features
- Complete end-to-end AI workflows for specific tasks (resume, cold email, code review)

**What does NOT belong here:**
- Tips that are already covered in official documentation and widely known
- Tricks that require expensive paid API access without free alternative
- Workflows that haven't been personally tested

**Primary Formulas:** F01 (AI Cheat Code), F09 (Three Step System), F06 (AI vs Human), F08 (Mind Blowing Transformation)
**Backup Formulas:** F04 (when workflow is tool-centered)
**Primary CTA:** Save ("Save karo"), DM trigger ("SYSTEM", "PROMPT")
**Primary Metric:** Save rate, DM volume

---

### Pillar 3 — AI CERTIFICATIONS & CAREER
*"The career move they're not making yet"*

**Definition:** Curated AI learning resources, certification paths, career positioning, and skill-building opportunities specifically relevant to Indian professionals navigating the AI transition. The creator's role here is the career advisor — filtering signal from noise in the overwhelmingly large AI education market.

**What belongs here:**
- Free and low-cost AI certifications with genuine career value (IBM, Google, Coursera, DeepLearning.AI)
- Role-specific AI skill guides (developer, designer, product manager, content creator)
- AI career urgency content — the "why now" argument with real data
- LinkedIn and resume optimization for AI roles
- Indian job market analysis for AI skills
- GitHub repos, newsletters, and communities worth following

**What does NOT belong here:**
- Paid courses without verified quality (no affiliation-driven lists)
- Certifications the creator hasn't personally reviewed
- Western-centric career advice that doesn't apply to Indian market
- Content that creates urgency without providing the action path

**Primary Formulas:** F05 (Career List), F02 (Future Shock)
**Backup Formulas:** F09 (Three Step System for learning workflows)
**Primary CTA:** Follow (@ai_snipp), DM trigger ("CERTIFICATIONS", "LIST")
**Primary Metric:** Follow rate, Save rate

---

### Pillar 4 — AI NEWS & UPDATES
*"The AI update that matters for you"*

**Definition:** Translation of significant AI developments into plain Hinglish with specific Indian-relevance angles. The creator's role here is the filter — the viewer's trusted friend who reads everything and tells them only what matters. Every news piece must have a "kya matlab hai tumhare liye" (what this means for you) angle.

**What belongs here:**
- Model releases and major capability upgrades (Claude, GPT, Gemini, Llama)
- AI company policy changes that affect Indian users (pricing, API access, free tiers)
- Regulatory developments in India or globally that affect AI users
- AI adoption data with Indian market angle
- Viral AI moments that need debunking or explanation
- "AI in the news" that the viewer saw headlines about but didn't understand

**What does NOT belong here:**
- News older than 48 hours (without explicitly acknowledging the delay)
- Technical research papers (unless there's a genuinely accessible finding)
- Western market news with zero Indian relevance
- Speculation framed as fact
- News the creator doesn't fully understand

**Primary Formulas:** F07 (News Flash), F10 (Comment React — community response to news)
**Backup Formulas:** F02 (Future Shock — when news creates career urgency)
**Primary CTA:** Follow (@ai_snipp), Save (for news that's reference material)
**Primary Metric:** Follow rate, Reach

### Imbalance Warning Signals

Watch for these weekly imbalances and correct:

| Signal | What It Means | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| 3+ F02 posts in one week | Fear fatigue — audience burning out on urgency | Add F09 (action path) + F05 (career hope) |
| No F10 in 14 days | Comment engagement dropping | React to a recent comment immediately |
| Only tool content (F01+F04) for 5+ days | Account looks like a tool aggregator | Add F09 (workflow) or F05 (career) |
| No F07 when major news breaks | Credibility gap as AI source | Drop everything, cover the news within 24h |
| Save rate declining | Too many Follow-CTA reels | Add 2× F09 or F06 this week (Save CTAs) |
| DM volume dropping | Too many Follow CTAs, not enough DM hooks | Run F04 or F01 with DM trigger |

## CONTENT PILLAR DECISION TREE

```
You have a content idea. Which pillar does it belong to?

Is it about a specific TOOL?
├── Yes, and the tool itself is the discovery → Pillar 1: AI TOOLS
└── No, the tool is just the mechanism for a shortcut/workflow...

Is it about a SHORTCUT, WORKFLOW, or TRICK?
├── Yes, how to get more from AI you already have → Pillar 2: AI CHEAT CODES
└── No...

Is it about AI CAREER or LEARNING RESOURCES?
├── Yes, certifications, skills, career path → Pillar 3: CERTIFICATIONS & CAREER
└── No...

Is it about AI NEWS or an AI DEVELOPMENT?
├── Yes, something that happened in AI → Pillar 4: AI NEWS & UPDATES
└── None of the above → Does not belong on @ai_snipp
```
