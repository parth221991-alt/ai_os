# Content Ideas Database
## AI_SNIPP Research & Intelligence System

*Every content idea that enters the system gets scored. Scored ideas get prioritized. Prioritized ideas get produced. Nothing gets produced ad-hoc. This database is the buffer between inspiration and execution — ensuring the best ideas surface first, and weak ideas are filtered before wasting production time.*

## IDEA SCORING FRAMEWORK

*Score every logged idea on 6 dimensions. Total score determines production priority tier.*

### Dimension Definitions and Scoring

**D1 — Audience Pain Intensity (1–5)**
*How acutely does the target audience feel this pain right now?*

| Score | Criteria |
|---|---|
| 5 | Pain observed in real comments this week — multiple instances, emotionally charged language |
| 4 | Pain documented in APIFY research or competitor comment buckets — recurring theme |
| 3 | Known general pain in AI learner/user community — not sourced from specific comments |
| 2 | Inferred pain — creator-observed, not directly validated by audience language |
| 1 | No clear pain — "interesting topic" without demonstrated audience need |

**D2 — Formula Fit Clarity (1–5)**
*How cleanly does this idea map to exactly one formula?*

| Score | Criteria |
|---|---|
| 5 | Maps perfectly to one formula's "Best for" list — no forcing required |
| 4 | Strong fit with minor stretching — clear primary formula |
| 3 | Two formulas could work — primary is chosen but backup exists |
| 2 | Formula is being adapted — some requirements of the chosen formula are missing |
| 1 | Doesn't cleanly fit any formula — needs new approach |

**D3 — Proof Quality (1–5)**
*How strong is the on-screen demonstration available?*

| Score | Criteria |
|---|---|
| 5 | Can demo on-screen with real numbers right now — no additional prep needed |
| 4 | Strong demo with 30–60 min of setup — real numbers, reproducible |
| 3 | Good demo but requires 2+ hours of prep or has one weak link |
| 2 | Can show concept but not with specific numbers — directional only |
| 1 | Commentary/opinion only — no on-screen proof possible |

**D4 — Indian Relevance (1–5)**
*How specifically does this matter to the Indian AI audience?*

| Score | Criteria |
|---|---|
| 5 | Directly affects Indian users specifically (pricing, access, Hindi feature, India career market) |
| 4 | High relevance — applies to Indian developers/students/creators in a meaningful way |
| 3 | Moderate — applies to Indian audience as part of global audience |
| 2 | Low — mostly Western context, limited Indian angle |
| 1 | No Indian relevance — purely US/European market content |

**D5 — Freshness (1–5)**
*For news/trend content: how time-sensitive? For evergreen: default to 3.*

| Score | Criteria |
|---|---|
| 5 | Breaking — news is < 6 hours old, no competitor has covered it |
| 4 | Fresh — 6–24 hours old, early coverage |
| 3 | Evergreen — not time-dependent (default for F01, F04, F05, F06, F08, F09, F10) |
| 2 | Slightly dated — 3–7 days old for trend content |
| 1 | Stale — more than 7 days for trend content (unless evergreen treatment) |

**D6 — Production Simplicity (1–5)**
*How easy is this to produce given current resources? Simpler = higher score.*

| Score | Criteria |
|---|---|
| 5 | F07 or F10 complexity — can produce in under 2 hours |
| 4 | F01 or F04 complexity — 3–4 hours, standard production |
| 3 | F05 or F09 complexity — 4–5 hours, some prep |
| 2 | F06 or F08 complexity — 5–6 hours, significant asset prep |
| 1 | F03 complexity — 6+ hours, output quality gate may delay |

### Priority Tiers

| Score | Tier | Label | Production Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|
| 27–30 | **P0** | Produce immediately | Within 4–8 hours — drop other work |
| 22–26 | **P1** | This week's priority | Within 24–48 hours |
| 16–21 | **P2** | Queue this week | Within 7 days |
| 10–15 | **P3** | Batch next week | Within 14 days |
| Below 10 | **Archive** | Not ready yet | Hold — re-evaluate in 30 days or drop |

**P0 trigger rule:** Any news content (F07, F02) that scores P1 should be upgraded to P0 — news content degrades with every hour of delay. A P1 news reel produced 24 hours later is worth half as much as a P0 news reel produced now.

## PRODUCTION PRIORITY FRAMEWORK

### Weekly Production Planning

At the start of each week (Sunday evening), sort the idea database by:

1. **P0 items first** — if any P0 exists (breaking news), it is produced Monday.
2. **P1 items by formula balance** — check formula balance against content_pillars.md weekly mix target. Select P1 items that correct any imbalances.
3. **P2 items by pillar** — fill remaining production slots with P2 items, ensuring all 4 pillars are represented.
4. **P3 items for future batch** — leave in queue, review again next week.

**Weekly production slot budget:**
| Slot | Day | Format | Source Pool |
|---|---|---|---|
| Slot 1 | Monday | Reel | P0 or top P1 |
| Slot 2 | Tuesday | Carousel | P1 or P2 |
| Slot 3 | Wednesday | Reel | P1 |
| Slot 4 | Thursday | Static or Reel | P2 (fast formula: F07 or F10) |
| Slot 5 | Friday | Reel | P1 |
| Slot 6 | Saturday | Carousel | P2 |
| Slot 7 | Sunday | Reel | P2 or P3 batch test |

### Fast-Track Rules

**Auto-fast-track to P0:**
- AI model release from major company (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google)
- Pricing change affecting Indian users
- Free tier announcement for a tool the audience uses
- Viral AI moment (1M+ impressions in 24 hours) that needs explanation/debunking

**Auto-archive:**
- Any idea where proof is "opinion only" (D3 = 1)
- Any news content that is now >72 hours old without production start
- Any idea that duplicates content produced in last 14 days
- Any idea where the creator hasn't personally tested the tool/workflow

## FORMULA RECOMMENDATION FRAMEWORK

*Given a content idea, use this matrix to find the right formula before scoring.*

### Asset-to-Formula Matrix

| You Have | Primary Formula | Backup Formula | Key Question to Decide |
|---|---|---|---|
| AI tool shortcut / hidden feature | F01 — AI Cheat Code | F04 — Hidden Tool | Is the TRICK the value, or is the TOOL the value? |
| New AI tool (< 6 months Indian awareness) | F04 — Hidden Tool | F01 — AI Cheat Code | Is the tool itself the discovery, or is a feature the discovery? |
| Impressive AI-generated video/image | F03 — Hollywood VFX | F08 — Transformation | Is the visual the hook, or is the quality-gap the hook? |
| Before/after quality transformation | F08 — Mind Transformation | F06 — AI vs Human | Is the gap about QUALITY or SPEED? |
| Time comparison (manual vs AI) | F06 — AI vs Human | F08 — Transformation | Is the primary stat TIME or QUALITY? |
| 3–5 verified resources or certifications | F05 — Career List | F09 — Three Step System | Is it a LIST of resources or a PROCESS using resources? |
| Breaking AI news (< 48 hours) | F07 — News Flash | F02 — Future Shock | Is it NEWS (what happened?) or URGENCY (what should viewer do now?)? |
| AI trend with career urgency | F02 — Future Shock | F07 — News Flash | Is the primary emotion FEAR of falling behind or need to KNOW what happened? |
| Complete 3-step workflow | F09 — Three Step System | F05 — Career List | Is it an ACTION workflow or a RESOURCE list? |
| Real viewer comment or question | F10 — Comment React | F09 — Three Step System | Is the question from a real viewer this week, or a general question? |

### Formula Override Rules

**Never use F02 (Future Shock) more than once in the same week** — track count before scheduling.

**F03 (Hollywood VFX) requires output quality gate** — the AI-generated visual must be evaluated as genuinely impressive before this formula is assigned. Do not assign F03 in the idea database; assign it only after the output has been generated and assessed.

**F07 (News Flash) has a 48-hour freshness gate** — ideas logged for F07 automatically downgrade to F02 or archive if they are not produced within 48 hours of the triggering news event.

**F10 (Comment React) requires a real comment** — the comment must exist before this formula is assigned. Do not create hypothetical comments.

## DATABASE MAINTENANCE

### Weekly Maintenance (Sunday, 10 min)

1. **Score any new ideas** captured during the week that haven't been scored yet
2. **Update status** of ideas in production (In Production / Published)
3. **Archive** any ideas that have been in P3 for 30+ days without production start
4. **Upgrade** any previously P2/P3 news ideas that have become relevant again due to new development
5. **Add** any new hooks or pain language to hook_library.md that were discovered this week

### Idea Database Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Captured | Logged, not yet scored |
| Scored | Scored, tier assigned |
| Queued | Assigned to a production slot |
| In Production | Content Request created, production started |
| Published | Reel is live — log REEL_ID |
| Archived | Scored below threshold or superseded — do not delete, re-evaluate in 30 days |
| Dropped | Definitively rejected — cannot be reactivated |
