## Why This Topic Was Selected

### The Audience Pain Is Intense and Universal

"LinkedIn post likhna chahta hun lekin pata nahi kya likhun" is one of the most recurring pain points across Indian professional communities — r/techindia, LinkedIn influencer comment sections, and competitor comment buckets all show the same pattern: Indian students, developers, and professionals know they need a LinkedIn presence, but blank-page paralysis kills it before it starts.

This is not a niche developer problem. Every student preparing for placements, every developer building a personal brand, every professional trying to get noticed — all feel this exact pain. Audience size is maximum.

### The Comparison is Shareable by Design

F06 (AI vs Human) is the highest-share formula in the library. But not all comparisons share equally. This one shares specifically because of the WhatsApp forwarding behavior of Indian professionals: "Bhai yeh dekh — LinkedIn post 4 minute mein!" gets forwarded to college batch groups, developer Slack channels, and placement prep WhatsApp groups. The task is relatable enough that forwarding feels like a gift to the receiver.

### Four-Target Optimization Analysis

| Target | Mechanism | Expected Signal |
|---|---|---|
| **Retention** | Timer visual ticking during AI demo. Before/after comparison holds viewer through Clip 4. "Views fark" stat (340 vs 1,200) is a second hook within the reel. | Watch completion > 70% |
| **Shares** | "Mere dost ko bhej do jiska LinkedIn blank pada hai" — the reel IS the gift. WhatsApp forwarding demographic matches perfectly. | Share rate above F06 baseline |
| **Comments** | Seeded debate: "AI-generated posts get flagged by LinkedIn" — a contested claim that drives "haan" / "nahi" comments. First comment from creator seeds this. | 30+ comments within 4 hours |
| **Follows** | Positions @ai_snipp as the source for AI productivity shortcuts — not just tool demos. Broadens appeal beyond developers to all professionals. | Net +50 followers within 48h |

### The Numbers Are Real and Defensible

45-minute vs 6-minute claims fail when the Indian tech audience tests them. These numbers reflect an actual test:
- Manual post: Blank-to-publish timer running during testing. 25 minutes is accurate for someone who cares about quality.
- Claude post: From "send prompt" to "approved, publishing" — 4 minutes including one round of light editing.
- View count comparison: Real, organic posts on the same account. 340 vs 1,200 is the actual delta from the creator's own content experiment.

The proof is specific, personal, and verifiable.
