## QUALITY STANDARDS

Verify before approving:

- Topic maps to exactly one formula's "Best for:" use case list
- Hook is in creator's Hinglish voice — not generic English, not press-release language
- Hook source is traceable (real audience language, real stat, real test)
- Proof is specific and generalizable
- CTA is singular — one action, one trigger word
- Topic is not a repeat of last 14 days (check reel log)
- For F07 (News Flash): topic is <48 hours old

---

## REJECTION CRITERIA

Reject this request if any of the following are true:

- Hook starts with "In this video..." or "Today I want to talk about..."
- Proof is a vague claim ("AI makes you faster") with no specific number
- Two CTAs competing in the same reel
- Formula is being forced onto a topic that doesn't fit its "Best for:" list
- Topic is a repeat without clear differentiation
- For F02: topic is manufactured urgency without a real trend
