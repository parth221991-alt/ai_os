## SYSTEM LOGIC NOTE

*Why this sequence works (the "why it matters" that F09 requires):*

Step 1 (Research) exists because personalization is the #1 factor in cold email response rate. But manual research takes 20 minutes per prospect — nobody does it consistently. AI cuts this to 3 minutes, making personalization scalable.

Step 2 (Personalization) turns the research output into a real email. The key is the prompt constraint: "3-line, direct value, no fluff." This produces output the sender actually sends, not output that requires 20 minutes of editing.

Step 3 (Test Loop) converts the system from "one good template" into a compounding asset. Each send generates data; Claude generates variations from the winner. After 20 sends, the sender has a genuinely optimized opening strategy for their specific audience — not a generic template.

The sequence is: Remove research friction (Step 1) → Remove writing friction (Step 2) → Build compounding signal (Step 3). Each step removes a different failure mode. That's why it's a system, not a tip.
